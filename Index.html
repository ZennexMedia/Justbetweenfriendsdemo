Just Between Friends Café

Welcome to Just Between Friends, your cozy spot in Manchester's Northern Quarter for exceptional coffee and a warm, minimalist atmosphere. We pride ourselves on sourcing meticulously selected beans from London's renowned Assembly roastery to bring you refined espresso and batch brews highlighting the finest origin character and roast precision.

Our Philosophy

At Just Between Friends, we believe in creating a community space where quality coffee and friendly faces meet. Our beans are carefully sourced from Assembly roastery in London, ensuring every cup you enjoy bursts with character and precision. Whether you're a local professional, student, or coffee aficionado, our café is designed to be your home away from home.
